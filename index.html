About ME

I’m a photographer based in Louisville, Kentucky, currently focusing on product photography. My work explores how careful lighting, composition, and attention to detail can highlight the character and quality of everyday objects. I originally studied information technology and discovered photography as a hobby. Over time my interest grew beyond simply capturing moments—I became more interested in designing the final image through lighting, staging, and technical control. Before focusing on product photography, I worked with family and portrait photography, experiences that helped shape my attention to detail and visual storytelling. Today I continue exploring how thoughtful image design can transform simple products into engaging photographs.
